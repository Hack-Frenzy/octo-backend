COVID Home Health Care System

Previous Next


As the number of cases of COVID continue to increase at an alarming rate, the number of beds in hospitals for isolation as well as quarantine of patients are becoming inadequate. This situation requires the immediate encouragement of the practices of home quarantine and isolation. To enable this medical practitioners require an application which would enable remote monitoring of patients. COVCare aims to be the medium to establish this mode of evaluation of patients.

COVCare comprises of mobile applications for patients and web application for hospitals for smooth conductance of home quarantine and isolation. The hospitals which register for this portal will be assigned a specific number of patients for monitoring. The patients will upload the necessary medical parameters which will be monitored by the registered physicians of the hospital. Home quarantined or L1 home isolated patients are the beneficiaries of this service.

Any government approved medical practitioner/organization/hospital/government body can register to be a monitoring doctor, however they should have the immediate reference contacts if the condition of the patient deteriorates. For treatment of COVID patients the hospital should have the necessary treatment facilities permit.
